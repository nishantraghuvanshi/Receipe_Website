Shahi Paneer

Shahi paneer is a popular North Indian dish that typically consists of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce with a blend of spices. The dish has a rich, smooth, and creamy texture with a mildly sweet and slightly tangy taste. The sauce is usually mildly spiced, with flavors of cumin, coriander, and garam masala, and the dish is often garnished with chopped fresh coriander leaves and sliced almonds or cashews.

Daal Makhani

Daal makhani is a popular North Indian dish that is made from a combination of whole black lentils (urad dal) and red kidney beans (rajma), slow-cooked with butter or cream, and a blend of aromatic spices. The dish has a rich, creamy texture and a slightly sweet and mildly spiced flavor.

Dum Aloo

Dum aloo is a popular Indian dish that is made with baby potatoes that are cooked in a spicy tomato-based sauce. The dish has a rich, slightly tangy, and mildly spiced flavor with a subtle sweetness.
